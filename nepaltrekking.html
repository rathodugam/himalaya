Trekking In Nepal

Nepal being the home of mountains is a paradise for trekkers all around the world. Home of 8 out of 10 highest mountains in the world, Nepal Trekking offers a glance of amazing beauty through the eyes of the Himalayas. The trek to Base Camp of the world’s highest mountain, Mount Everest is the dream journey for most adventure lovers. Engulfed in the biodiversity, there are lots of options for the people who like to trek.From the popular short treks that can be achieved in a week like Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek, Mardi Himal Base Camp trek to the trek that lasts for longer than 10 days like Everest Base Camp (EBC) Trek, Annapurna Base Camp (ABC) Trek can be done in Nepal. The trekking trails offers a glamorous beauty of sunrise and sunsets, clear skies, clear views are too beautiful to not be seen. The mountain trails for trekking are too popular and regular that food and accommodations will not be a problem in the trek.The biodiversity in the trekking routes can be noticed significantly. Every trekking route has a different local and ethnic culture residing in.If you are not in the mountains, then you can do the hiking in the hills that are below 4000m. In Nepal, peaks below 4000m are called hills. Whether you are in the city or in the hills, you can always do trekking and hiking wherever you fancy. If you are in Kathmandu you can hike up to Nagarjuna Dandna, ShivaPuri Danda and many other hills to climb up in a day. Also, if you are in Pokhara then you can visit the Naudanda Sarangkot or Santi Gumba.A lot of options available, whether you want a short trek of a week or mediocre trek about 15 days or long trek for a month. We can find any genre of a trek from challenging and difficult to moderate and easy treks in Nepal.
